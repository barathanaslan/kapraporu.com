Share Buyback Program Announcement
Finansal

Turkish Airlines (THYAO) is initiating a new share buyback program, subject to shareholder approval at the upcoming general assembly. The program aims to support the stock's value, protect shareholders, and contribute to price stability.

The Board of Directors has decided to propose a share buyback program to the shareholders at the next general assembly. The program will be valid for 3 years from the date of the general assembly approval. The maximum amount allocated for the buyback is 9,000,000,000 TL, sourced from the company's cash portfolio. The maximum number of shares to be bought back is limited to 1.7% of the company's issued capital.

General Assembly Information Regarding Procedures
Yatırım

Turkish Airlines (THYAO) is seeking shareholder approval for a new share buyback program at the upcoming General Assembly on May 21, 2025. The proposed program aims to support the stock's value and protect shareholders.

The General Assembly will convene on May 21, 2025, to discuss and vote on the proposed share buyback program. The agenda includes the approval of the new share buyback program prepared by the Board of Directors. Shareholders will also receive information about previous share buybacks conducted under the framework of Capital Markets Board regulations. The announcement includes attachments with the agenda, information document, and the share buyback program details.
